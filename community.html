Our Community Programs

Transforming research into real-world impact through grassroots action

🤰 Maternal Health

Maternal Health Workshops

Empowering pregnant women and new mothers with life-saving knowledge, danger sign recognition, and nutrition advice.

  • Monthly sessions in 15+ communities
  • 2,500+ women reached annually
  • Partnership with local clinics
👩‍⚕️ Healthcare Training

Health Worker Training

Advanced diagnostic and treatment protocols for placental diseases, delivered through hands-on certification courses.

  • 500+ healthcare workers trained
  • Certification & CPD points
  • Blended learning + mentorship
🏘️ Community Outreach

Mobile Health Clinics

Bringing screening, diagnostics, and health education directly to remote villages.

  • 12 mobile clinics per year
  • Free malaria & syphilis screening
  • Follow-up care coordination
