Major Breakthrough in Placental Malaria Research Published in Nature Medicine

Our team's groundbreaking study on the single-cell atlas of malaria-infected placenta has been published in Nature Medicine, marking a significant milestone in understanding placental malaria pathology. This research opens new avenues for therapeutic interventions and could save thousands of maternal and infant lives across Africa.
